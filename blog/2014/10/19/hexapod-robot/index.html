Hexapod Robot

Oct 19th, 2014 3:00 pm

Hexapod Robot

Inspired by the prosperity and variety of the contemporary field of hexapod robot, our group had decided to make one that can move forward and backward. There are many types of hexapod robots and we decided to start with the simplest, therefore a design which is based on four-link mechanism was incorporated in our final design.

We started this project from scratch. After doing much researching, we got to know more about the state of art and in the end we came up with a design filled with our teammatesâ€™ ingenuity and creativity. Based on the design, we started the assembly. Our research method is mainly hands-on, which means we find a problem when we are tinkering and come up with a solution. The main ways of manufacturing the parts involves lathe turning, drilling, metal bending, sawing, mechanical cutting, laser cutting, soldering, adhesive and mechanical fastenings including screws and bolts, clamp spring restraining. Due to the fact that we were lack of engineering experiences and knowledge, we ran into many difficulties. Thanks to the teachers in the engineering training center, we solved all the problems successfully despite all the stumbles during the process.

In the end, our team had manufactured a hexapod that can move forward and backward, and turn left or right. The basic structure of the robot consists of two parts: mechanical part and electrical part. The mechanical part involves the legs, the body and the gears; the electrical part includes the motors, the circuit and the battery. Together, they bring the hexapod robot into life.

The four-link mechanism makes it possible for this robot to move with six legs driven by only two motors. Compared to other kinds of hexapod robots, this robot is definitely primitive, yet there is beauty lies in its simplicity.

Keywords: hexapod robot, four-link mechanism, machining process technology
